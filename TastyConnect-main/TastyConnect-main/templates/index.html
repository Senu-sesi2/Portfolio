Introducing TastyConnect

Your gateway to an extensive collection of traditional and contemporary African recipes, shared by passionate home cooks and food enthusiasts

TastyConnect was inspired by the developers' mutual love for food and the under-representation of African cuisine on the international scene. The team behind this project consists of MaryAnn Omenuko Emmanuel Mensah and Emelda Mada .This is a project portfolio for ALX.SE and Holberton Schoool.

Features and Highlights

  • Discover mouthwatering dishes from North Africa to Southern Africa, from East Africa to West Africa, and everywhere in between.

  • Showcase traditional cooking methods, local ingredients, and cultural significance to ensure an enriching culinary experience.

  • Share your family's treasured dishes, experiment with unique flavors, and connect with fellow food lovers who share your passion for African cuisine
