Kushari

Kushari, Koshary or Koshari is an Egyptian national dish. It can be served as a main course or it can be found as street food.

Main Ingredients consist of rice, lentils, macaroni, tomato sauce, onions etc

Sadza

Sadza is a staple in Zimbabwe and other Southern parts but with a different name. It is made from cornmeal and is accompaniment to many vegetable and meat dishes.

Main ingredients include cornmeal, water, millet and selected ground grain.
